The palettes.txt file contains 15 * 8 24-bit values.
each group of 15 are the RGB values for one particular palette.
the palettes are in the following order
Background	- 0-7 contain colors used in the background, 8-15 are blank (000000)
ducks		- 0-15 are colors used to draw ducks
char 1		- 1,3,5,7,9,11,13,15 are white, all others are black
char 2		- 2,3,6,7,10,11,14,15 are white, all others are black
char 3		- 4,5,6,7,12,13,14,15 are white, all others are black
char 4		- 8 to 15 are white, all others are black
blank sprite	- 0 is black, all others are white
blank bg	- all are black
